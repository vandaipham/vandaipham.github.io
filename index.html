PhD. Student

Bonch-Bruevich Saint-Petersburg State University of Telecommunications

I am a PhD. student focusing on info-communication technologies at the Bonch-Bruevich Saint-Petersrbug University of Telecommunications. My research is on self-organized wireless mesh network for IoT devices in Smart Substainable Cities.

I received my MSc and BSc in infocummunication technologies and communication systems from the SPbSUT in 2017 and 2019, respectively. During time at SPbSUT, I participated as IoT R&D in the IoTLab at the department of Communication Networks and Data Transmission. At the end of master’s and bachelor’s education, I was awarded an exellence in graduate award from the SPbSUT. My research has been published in Lecture Notes in Computer Science, IEEE Explore, and other peer-reviewed journals.
